ABOUT THE TEAM

Gabriella

I have always loved leather goods and wanted to be involved in making them. However, 3 years ago I discovered my true passion - carving on leather. Since then, not a day passes without me engraving and developing in this area. Along with the engravings, I also started to draw more so that I could give them an even more realistic look. This is basically my part of the "job" in our great team - to give individuality to each piece and make it unique and above all personal for its future owner.

Todor

I'm self-taught. I got into leather crafting over 10 years ago. I developed, searched for information, tried, failed, and tried again to reach the master level with great difficulty. Then I found Gabriella and passed on my knowledge and skills - I lit her up too. Now we work together like a well-oiled machine. She mainly engraves and paints, and I design the piece, cut, sew, finish the edges... in fact, everything to turn several pieces into one functional and beautiful product that you will enjoy wearing.

Who does what

First is Gabriella, she`s starting with checking her facebook and telephone for new orders.

She has an order, the next step for her is to start engraving the image that it`s send to her from the customer.

After she is done with the engraving it`s time for the paint and the drawing. And the last part for her is to put special paste on top of the paint.

It came time for Todor! His first job is to thin the edges out, after he`s done with that he is enclosing the corners of the product, glues them and sews them up.

After she is done with the engraving it`s time for the paint and the drawing. And the last part for her is to put special paste on top of the paint.
